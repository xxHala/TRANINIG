The Journey to the Lake

I still remmber going fishing with my grandfather early in the morning to the lake. He would wake me up before daylight and we would hop into his old ford truck. The morning breeze would gently make me by the time we arrived at the lake. Once there,he would secure my life-vest and we would jump into the silver boat and head out to his favorite stop. I don't remmber the conversations that much, but I always enjyoed it just being the two of us and catching the fish .

The cooking

No matter what we caught,large or small my grandmother would clean it ,season it and have it fried by lunch time I wish I had asked her about recipes then, by hindsight is always by 20/20. This is why I figured I would merge programming skills and create a Recipe using HTML lists.
